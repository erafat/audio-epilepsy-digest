What to review

Listen to any one of the seven published AED episodes, then use the public source-review index if you want to inspect how the episode handled evidence and uncertainty.

What we ask

  • Educational relevance for epilepsy training.
  • Whether limitations and uncertainty were preserved.
  • How useful the audio format is compared with written digests.
  • What should change in future episodes.

How it is used

Responses are reviewed in aggregate and folded into future production decisions: topic framing, episode length, source-review disclosure, and optional teaching materials.
